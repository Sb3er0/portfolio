The project planning department of HBS Automation GmbH offers professional planning, drawing and documentation for switchgear of all kinds.

HBS Automation plans and constructs switchgear using eplan Electric P8 and WS -CAD. From simple installation distributors via control table and desks to highly complex automation systems, everything is possible.
